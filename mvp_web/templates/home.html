Turn your bank & wallet exports into a monthly summary

This tool ingests spreadsheets and PDFs, normalizes transactions, applies your category rules, and builds simple budget metrics. Everything runs in a temporary browser session—nothing permanent unless you download the outputs.

Supported today: Mercado Pago account exports and Santander Argentina (checking account movements and Visa card statements), as XLSX or PDF. Other banks or formats are not wired in yet—results may be wrong or empty.

  1. Download your latest Santander movements, your card statement(s), and your Mercado Pago activity from each provider’s website or app (whatever they give you as XLSX/PDF).

  2. Upload those files below. As soon as you confirm in the file dialog, they are sent automatically—no extra click needed.

  3. Process to merge everything, categorize, and compute metrics. All movements in your files are included; the output filename uses the month of the latest transaction date (not a filter).

  4. Optional: tweak category rules (keywords + regex) and run Process again to refine labels.

Why regex in the JSON? Each category can list keywords (simple substrings) and regex patterns. Regex lets you match messy descriptions—e.g. merchant names with extra numbers, or “PAGO VISA *1234”. The pipeline tests your transaction text against those patterns so recurring merchants land in the right bucket. Edit rules under Advanced below.
